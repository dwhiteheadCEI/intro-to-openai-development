A seemingly gentle soul who serves as the head housekeeper at John Hargrove's estate. She looks to be in her mid 60s, her pale complexion shows she has been through quite a number of stories. Her demeanor is warm and her smile ever-present, making her a favored member of the household staff. However, beneath her kind exterior, she engages in mysterious activities that have not gone unnoticed, especially her frequent, secretive outings and unexplained late-night phone calls. Her background remains shrouded in secrecy, with little known about her past before she appeared at the mansion's doorstep seeking employment. Despite her suspicious actions, Her impeccable service and discreet nature have kept her above reproach, leaving many to wonder about her true intentions.
