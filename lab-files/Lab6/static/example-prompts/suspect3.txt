The youngest son of Jack Hargrove, age 32. He embodies the family's famed intelligence and his father's irresistible charisma, yet channels these traits into his extravagant socialite lifestyle. Known throughout the city's elite for his lavish parties and flamboyant persona, he always ensures he is the center of attention, often ending up in the public limelight in trouble with his wild antics. Despite his ostentatious behavior, he possesses a sharp mind and a knack for charming his way out of any predicament. While his sister builds an empire, his pursuits seem frivolous, yet some speculate that his reckless facade masks more calculated, selfish endeavors. His life of excess, wrapped in charm and mystery, keeps everyone guessing about his true role in the family legacy.
